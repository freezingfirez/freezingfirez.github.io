Fisch Apps – Support

Welcome to the official support page for all apps developed by Nathan Fischer, including Simply Click, GlobalTag, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How can I get support for an app?
    A: You can reach out anytime by emailing nathan@fischdesigns.com. Please include the name of the app and any details about your issue.
  • Q: Where can I find your apps?
    A: All published apps are available on the iOS App Store. Search for the app name (e.g., “Simply Click” or “GlobalTag”) or visit the [App Store developer page](https://apps.apple.com/developer/nathan-fischer/id6749104273) when available.
  • Q: I found a bug or want to suggest a feature.
    A: I’d love to hear from you. Please send feedback to nathan@fischdesigns.com.
  • Q: Do your apps collect my data?
    A: No personal data is collected or shared without your consent. Location-based features (like in GlobalTag) operate only on your device. For more details, view each app’s Privacy Policy in the App Store listing.

Stay Updated

New features, updates, and upcoming projects are always in development. Follow along by checking the App Store or visiting this page again soon.
