Simply Click - Support

Welcome to the support page for Simply Click, a clicker-style game created by Nathan Fischer.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: What is Simply Click?
    A: Simply Click is a SwiftUI-based game where you tap to gain points and reach milestones. More features like a shop and leaderboard are planned.
  • Q: Where can I download it?
    A: Currently, Simply Click is in development and not yet on the App Store.
  • Q: How do I report bugs or request features?
    A: You can contact me directly via email at nathan@fischdesigns.com.

Need More Help?

If your question wasn’t answered above, feel free to email me or check back later for updates as development progresses.
